What is BDNS? Introducing Blockchain Domain
Name Service (BDNS)

BDNS is the world's first Ethereum name-only domain name service. BDNS are mintable in the form non-fungible tokens (NFTs) which you can configure to an Ethereum wallet address on a purely name-only basis. For example, instead of "Blockchain.eth", your Ethereum wallet address becomes simply "Blockchain" with the purchase and configuration of a BDNS NFT.

Block functionality of the project
and benefits for the user.

  • 1

    Location, Location, Location!

    In the real world economy, addresses matter - and that's true for the virtual world economy too. BDNS NFTs elicit the potential to configure your Ethereum wallet address to the most prestigious names on the Blockchain. Over time, the exclusivity of a BDNS NFT address will translate into a better value investment NFT than any other around, the same way as a prestigious Tier 1 city address has in the physical world.

  • 2

    Fair Pricing

    BDNS NFTs are not priced by traits, as for most standard NFTs - they are priced by supply and industrial demand, as in the real world economy. This means that for most BNDS NFTs, the cost is a standard $9 (plus 70% discount on multiple years' registration if purchased upfront), making BDNS the most fairly priced NFT mint around today.

  • 3

    First Come, First Serve Policy

    There is no private sale and no privileged minting of BDNS NFTs that would otherwise disadvantage customers of BDNS, except in a very limited number of instances (such as with select brand names, which are reserved aside for a period for their owners). BDNS operates exclusively on a first-come, first-serve minting policy. Get minting before others grab your preferred address!

  • 4

    BDNS Is Building For The Long Term

    Most NFT products involve simply building a virtual token minting factory and leaving the bulk of work to the customer and the market. With BDNS it's different. BDNS developers are actively working on new Blockchain solutions that will be rolled out consistently to reinforce and revolutionize the value of BDNS NFTs.
